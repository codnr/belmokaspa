About Me

Sergely, also known as Mocha, has been in the fashion industry for almost ten years. She has worked in many facets of that industry which include styling, wholesale representative, and modeling. She has had to develop a skin regimen and diet that combines her natural esthetic with her holistic philosophy. A few years ago, she decided to enter the beauty industry in order to help people through skincare and wellness. While still maintaining a career in the fashion industry as model. She graduated top of her class from Aveda Institute New York from the esthetician program. She has been an esthetician for about three years, and has worked at different spas while also seeing private clients. She has gained great knowledge and confidence while working at Massage Envy, Oasis Day Spa, and her private clients. She is transitioning to her own space so that she can provide more to her clients and her community. She specializes in a more holistic, organic, and technology-guided approach to beauty and wellness.
